How this works & tips

Open this file any day (bookmark it). It auto-shows the current week. Tap a session → tick Done and log your actual distance, time and effort (1–10). Data is saved in this browser.

To plan with Claude: hit Copy data for Claude and paste it into chat — or Download backup and tell Claude to read ~/Downloads/comrades-progress.json.

Paces are built around a goal marathon pace of 6:30–7:00/km (a sub-4:42 to sub-5:00 qualifier) and slow down for distance. They're guides — effort wins over the watch. Easy runs must feel conversational; if a pace forces you to breathe hard, slow down, especially in the early weeks. Walk breaks are a strategy. A niggle that changes your gait = stop.
